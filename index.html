About the Shop

Welcome to The GY6 Scooter Repair Shop. We built this shop for riders who want focused GY6 work without the runaround. Whether your scooter needs basic service, a hard-start diagnosis, clutch work, fuel system repair, or performance parts installed, we work to get it handled right and get you back on the road with confidence.

What We Work On

We specialize in GY6 49cc and 150cc 4-stroke gas scooters. That includes routine maintenance, electrical troubleshooting, fuel delivery issues, starting problems, drivetrain repairs, and upgrade installs.

Why Riders Choose Us

Honest service, focused GY6 knowledge, and work aimed at reliability, performance, and ride quality. We are here to keep your scooter responsive, safe, and ready to roll.
