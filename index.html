About the Shop

Welcome to The GY6 Scooter Repair Shop. We are built for riders who need dependable GY6 service without the guesswork. Whether your scooter needs a tune-up, a no-start diagnosis, clutch work, fuel system repair, or performance parts installed, we focus on getting the problem handled correctly and getting you back on the road.

We specialize in GY6 49cc and 150cc 4-stroke gas scooters. That includes routine maintenance, electrical troubleshooting, fuel delivery issues, starting problems, drivetrain repairs, and upgrade installs.
